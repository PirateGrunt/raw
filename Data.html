Data sets

Below you will find several data sets which we will use during the workshop.

  • Hurricanes

    Hurricanes.csv

    This is data taken from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Adiministration. The file contains latitude and longitude by day for all hurricanes tracked by the NOAA. Information exists for over 150 years of observation. Hurricane name, wind speed and barometric pressure are also recorded.

  • NFL results

    NFL.csv

    This data has been taken from the site pro-football-reference.com. The data has been modified from its original form. This data set is a record of the results of over 7,000 NFL games. Fields include date of game, score, first downs, total yards, rushing and passing yards and number of turnovers.
